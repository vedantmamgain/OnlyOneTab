# Privacy Policy for OnlyOneTab

**Last Updated:** November 22, 2024

## Overview
OnlyOneTab is a browser extension designed to automatically manage duplicate browser tabs, helping users maintain a cleaner and more efficient browsing experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Our Privacy Commitment
**OnlyOneTab operates entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data to external servers.**

## Data Collection and Usage

### What OnlyOneTab Monitors
- **Tab URLs**: The extension monitors the URLs of your open tabs solely to identify duplicates from the same domain
- **Domain Patterns**: User-configured patterns to determine which websites should be managed

### What OnlyOneTab Stores
All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:
- Your tracking mode preference (all domains or specific patterns)
- Custom domain patterns you've configured
- Grouping preferences for how duplicates are handled

### What OnlyOneTab Does NOT Do
- ❌ Collect personal information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Track or store your browsing history
- ❌ Send any data to external servers
- ❌ Share data with third parties
- ❌ Use data for advertising or analytics
- ❌ Access the content of web pages
- ❌ Store data outside your local browser

## Permissions Explained

### `tabs` Permission
Required to detect when multiple tabs from the same domain are open. This permission allows the extension to:
- Read tab URLs to identify duplicates
- Switch to existing tabs when duplicates are detected
- Close duplicate tabs based on your preferences

### `storage` Permission
Required to save your preferences locally in your browser. This permission allows the extension to:
- Remember your tracking mode (all domains vs. specific patterns)
- Store your custom domain patterns
- Persist settings across browser sessions

## Data Retention
- All settings and preferences remain in your browser's local storage
- Data is only removed when you:
  - Uninstall the extension
  - Clear your browser data
  - Reset the extension settings

## Security
- All data processing occurs locally on your device
- No network requests are made to external servers
- No third-party scripts or services are used
- The extension operates in a sandboxed browser environment

## User Control
You have complete control over your data:
- Add or remove domain patterns at any time
- Switch tracking modes instantly
- Export your settings for backup
- Import settings from a backup
- All changes take effect immediately and are stored locally

## Children's Privacy
OnlyOneTab does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.
